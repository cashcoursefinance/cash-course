Who We Are

Cash Course (a play on words for crash course) is a nonprofit dedicated to spreading financial literacy to young adults. We tackle topics that school doesn't prepare you for — in a space where no question is too basic and no situation is too awkward to discuss.

25% of young adults don't have a trusted adult to turn to for financial guidance. We're here to change that — one module at a time.
